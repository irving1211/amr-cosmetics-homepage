## 10 — FAQ

**Section eyebrow:** GOOD TO KNOW

**Section headline:**
Questions, answered.

**Q1 — When will my order ship?**
Orders are packed within 1–3 business days. You'll receive tracking by email as soon as your package is on its way.

**Q2 — Do you ship internationally?**
Yes — we ship to most countries. Shipping options and rates appear at checkout based on your address.

**Q3 — What is your return policy?**
Unopened products can be returned within 14 days of delivery. For hygiene reasons, opened lip and hair products cannot be returned. Reach out and we'll take care of you.

**Q4 — Are AMR products safe for sensitive skin and scalps?**
Our formulas are designed to feel comfortable for most skin types. We always recommend a patch test before first use. If you have a known sensitivity, check the ingredients on each product page.

**Q5 — Can I use the hair oil daily?**
Yes — the hair oil is light enough for daily wear. Many love it as a finishing oil on dry hair; it also works as a pre-wash treatment on damp ends.

**Q6 — How do I reach you?**
Email us at hello@amrcosmetics.com — we read every message and reply quickly.

## Voice guardrails (for future edits)

- "Considered" beats "premium". Use "considered, never complicated".
- "Healthy-looking" — never "grows hair", "regrows", "stops loss", "repairs damage", "anti-aging".
- "Soft", "polished", "quiet", "in-between", "everyday" — yes.
- "Game-changing", "revolutionary", "secret", "magic", "miracle" — no.
- Sentences ≤ 22 words. If one runs longer, break it.
- One CTA per moment. Two only when paired (Hair / Lip).
- No exclamation marks anywhere on the homepage.
