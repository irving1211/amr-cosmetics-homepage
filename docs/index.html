GOOD TO KNOW

Questions, answered.

When will my order ship?

Orders are packed within 1–3 business days. You'll receive tracking by email as soon as your package is on its way.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes — we ship to most countries. Shipping options and rates appear at checkout based on your address.

What is your return policy?

Unopened products can be returned within 14 days of delivery. For hygiene reasons, opened lip and hair products cannot be returned. Reach out and we'll take care of you.

Are AMR products safe for sensitive skin and scalps?

Our formulas are designed to feel comfortable for most skin types. We always recommend a patch test before first use. If you have a known sensitivity, check the ingredients on each product page.

Can I use the hair oil daily?

Yes — the hair oil is light enough for daily wear. Many love it as a finishing oil on dry hair; it also works as a pre-wash treatment on damp ends.

How do I reach you?

You can reach us through the contact form, or via Instagram at @amr.cosmeticss.
